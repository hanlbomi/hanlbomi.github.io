About Me

I am a Sr. postdoctoral researcher at the University of Chicago, specializing in observational astronomy. My research focuses on massive data reduction (image processing and stellar photometry), along with statistical analysis of stellar photometric properties. My background is diverse, having grown up in East Asia (S. Korea) and spent four years in Europe (Germany), and settled down in the US (in the Windy City!). My professional interests lie in data analysis, and I have recently developed a keen interest in deep learning.

I enjoy drinking coffee and beer, and traveling all around the world. Please don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.
